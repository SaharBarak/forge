# Brand Context

## Name
- English: Taro
- Hebrew: תַּרְאוּ

## Tagline
- Hebrew: ריבונות אזרחית מתחילה כאן
- English: Civic sovereignty starts here

## Values
- Transparency
- Community empowerment
- Local action
- Honest experimentation
- Collective power

## Tone
- Direct but warm
- Hopeful but realistic
- Empowering, not preachy
- Israeli (informal, direct)
- Experimental, not promising

## Avoid
- Corporate speak
- Overpromising
- Condescending language
- Political jargon
- Abstract philosophy
- "Democracy" as a buzzword

## Key Messages
1. Small actions accumulate into real power
2. 3 shekels per vote builds a community fund
3. This is an experiment - we're honest about uncertainty
4. Local change is achievable
5. You're not alone in wanting change
