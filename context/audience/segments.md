# Audience Segments

## Primary Audience
**Tivon Residents (18-65)**
- Mix of young professionals and established families
- Politically diverse but share local concerns
- Frustrated with Facebook group chaos
- Want to be heard but skeptical of politics

## Pain Points
- "No one listens to regular people"
- "I'm alone in my frustration"
- "Facebook groups are chaos, not solutions"
- "I don't have time for committees and meetings"
- "Politicians promise but nothing changes"
- "I want to do something but don't know what"

## Desires
- To be heard by decision-makers
- To see concrete local change
- To feel part of a community
- To act without big time commitment
- To trust the process
