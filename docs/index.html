What the agents do

Research

Agents search the web for competitor copy, industry stats, and audience insights to back up their arguments.

Debate

Dialectic, Socratic, collaborative, or adversarial. They argue using real methodologies until they reach consensus.

Write

Each agent is a master copywriter. They know conversion psychology, visual hierarchy, and web copy best practices.

Loop until done

They don't stop after one round. Critique ‚Üí rewrite ‚Üí critique ‚Üí rewrite until every persona signs off.
